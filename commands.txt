Help Menu: .help links you to this document.
 
Stock: .stock 'Ticker Symbol' i.e. '.stock TSLA' will provide you with a stock quote.

Ask: .ask '.ask are cargo shorts cool', use or between choices for the bot to select a choice '.ask apple or orange'.

Praise: .praise '.praise USERNAME'.

Time: .time '.time Dublin, Ireland'.

Roll the Dice: .rtd '.rtd 20' will roll a 20 sided die.

Quotes: .quote pulls a random quote from the database, .quote 1 pulls quote 1 etc., .addquote '.addquote Thrusty: HI GUYS' will add that quote to the database."

Wiki: .wiki searchs wikipedia and provides a 2 sentance summary of the page. '.wiki Canada'.
